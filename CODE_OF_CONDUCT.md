# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

This is a private development project. All contributors are expected to:

- Communicate professionally and constructively
- Respect approved product decisions even when disagreeing
- Follow the documentation-first process — propose changes through RFCs, not unilateral implementation
- Report issues and mismatches honestly rather than silently patching them

## Product Conduct

Specific to Lookla:

- Do not introduce fake data, fake ratings, or fake activity of any kind
- Do not implement features that have not been approved through the RFC process
- Do not expose monetization, subscriptions, or advertising without explicit product approval
- Do not silently resolve conflicts between documentation and implementation
